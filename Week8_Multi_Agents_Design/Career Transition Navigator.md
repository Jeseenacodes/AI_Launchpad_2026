

# 🚀 Career Transition Navigator
The **Career Transition Navigator** is a conceptual multi-agent AI career coach that helps professionals transition into Data Analyst roles. By coordinating specialized agents for resume optimization, skills gap analysis, portfolio building, interview preparation, job market research, and LinkedIn branding, the system creates personalized career roadmaps tailored to each user's goals.

## 🚀 Problem

Career changers often struggle to understand which skills to learn, what projects to build, and how to position themselves for analyst roles.

This system creates personalized roadmaps using specialized AI agents.
